When it comes to dog breeds at Nk's Kennel, we provide you with a wide range of dog breeds for which you can choose one that will suit you.


Wide range of dog breeds includes;

  • Chihuahua

    • The charms of the Chihuahua dog breed include its small size, big personality, and wide range of coat kinds and colors. They're all dogs, capable of competing in dog sports like agility and obedience, and experts recommend them as one of the top 10 watchdogs. Despite the fact that these are purebred dogs, they can still be found at shelters and rescues. Keep in mind to adopt! If you want to bring a dog home, don't go shopping. Chihuahuas adore being with their owners — even inexperienced pet parents — and require very little maintenance and exercise. They make wonderful apartment dogs who get along with everyone in the household. Simply ensure that any children that approach you are familiar with how to play gently with a tiny dog.

  • Doberman Pinchers

    • Doberman Pinschers were developed in Germany in the late 1800s, mostly as security dogs. Although its actual history is uncertain, they are said to be a combination of several canine breeds, including the Rottweiler, Black and Tan Terrier, and German Pinscher. This puppy has the impression of an aristocracy, with its silky coat, athletic physique, and regal demeanor. They're high-energy, intelligent dogs who excel in police and military service, as well as canine sports and as family protectors and pets.

  • German Shepherd

    • For good reason, the German Shepherd Dog is one of America's most popular dog breeds. They're capable and intelligent working dogs. Their dedication and bravery are unrivaled. They're also incredibly adaptable. Alsatian is another name for the breed. German Shepherds, despite their purebred status, can be found at shelters and breed-specific rescues. So don't forget to adopt! If this is the breed for you, don't go shopping.

  • Great Dane

    • Oh, sweetheart! A Great Dane is a magnificent canine breed, huge and regal and affectionately known as the "Apollo of dogs. " Apollo is the Greek deity of the sun, the brightest celestial object. Great Danes have been present for thousands of years, and artifacts depicting Dane-like canines stretch back thousands of years. Despite the fact that this is a pure breed of dog, you can find them at shelters and rescues, therefore adopt! If you've decided this is the dog for you, don't go shopping.

  • Mastiff

    • The Mastiff is one of the most ancient types of dog breeds. Their ancestor, the Molossus, was known 5,000 years ago. Back then, they were ferocious war dogs, very different from the benevolent behemoth that the breed is today. Mastiffs are sometimes called Old English Mastiffs. Although this is a purebred dog, you may find them in shelters and rescues. Remember to adopt! Don’t shop if this is the breed for you.
